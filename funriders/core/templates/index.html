About Us

Where every day is filled with joy and celebration

Funriders Leisure & Amusement Pvt. Ltd., ISO 9001-2015 & ISO 14001-2015 certified Indoor and Outdoor play equipment manufacturer, started its magnificent voyage in 2010 with Outdoor playground equipment under the brand name of Buildindie ... Over the years, we acquired the acclamation as the market leader in this sector by providing various unparalleled designs and setting up superior children’s outdoor playground equipment in public and private parks, residential projects, schools, clubs, NGOs, and more. This success has strengthened our perspective and assisted us in expanding into wider platforms of play, including Family Entertainment Centres (FEC) and Kids Amusement Parks.
